Yao XIN received PhD degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2015. He was also a visiting research scholar of the University of Southern California, USA, in 2014. His research interests include network intelligent hardware acceleration, VLSI design for deep learning, and network algorithms.
