Ting Yang is currently an undergraduate student majoring in Microelectronics at Nankai University. She joins Prof. Ray's team as a summer research intern from July to September. She has worked on projects such as the architecture design of transimpedance amplifiers (TIA) for co-packaged optics (CPO).

Her primary research interests lie in high-speed optical communication systems, with a particular focus on bridging analog front-end circuits and digital signal processing architectures. During her summer internship in Prof. Ray Cheung's group, she aims to further explore the hardware mapping of advanced DSP algorithms on FPGA and ASIC platforms and develop a comprehensive system-level understanding of next-generation optical interconnects.
