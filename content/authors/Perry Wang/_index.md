Perry Wang is an undergraduate student in Integrated Circuit Design and Integrated Systems at the South China University of Technology. His research interests include FPGA/ASIC design, RISC-V and processor architecture, and digital circuit design.

He has experience in digital circuit performance analysis based on transistor characteristics, including delay and energy modeling, logical effort theory, and circuit optimization under design constraints. His recent work includes the design and publication of a delay-optimized 4-bit absolute value detector for embedded signal processing. He is also interested in efficient hardware design methodologies for embedded and integrated systems.
