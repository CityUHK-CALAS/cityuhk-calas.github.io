        <div style="text-align: justify">
        To further enhance the development of gifted education in Hong Kong, the Education Bureau set up the <strong>Gifted Education Fund</strong> in 2016 for generating investment income to support the service enhancement of the Hong Kong Academy for Gifted Education, to implement measures recommended by the Advisory Committee on Gifted Education, and to encourage gifted education providers to provide quality advanced learning programmes for gifted students.
        
        The GEF offers funding support for different organisations to provide <strong>off-school advanced learning programmes</strong> to expose gifted students to high quality and challenging learning experiences in their areas of talent or, where suitable, in a wider range of disciplines or even across disciplines.
        
        <strong>CityUHK Architecture Laboratory for Arithmetic and Security (CALAS)</strong> is proud to be an approved programme provider since 2020, offering hands-on research experiences in cutting-edge areas including RISC-V architecture, cryptographic hardware, AI hardware design, and embedded systems. Our most recent programme (Programme No. <strong>2025-06</strong>) for the 2025/26 school year focuses on <strong>Generative AI and Cybersecurity (GenAIC) Engineering Skills for Gifted Students Education</strong>.
        </div>

        <div style="text-align: justify">
        Our GEF programme offers gifted students exposure to cutting-edge research in multiple domains:
        
        - <strong>RISC-V Architecture</strong>: Explore open-source processor design and computer architecture
        - <strong>Cryptographic Hardware</strong>: Learn about hardware security, post-quantum cryptography, and secure system design
        - <strong>AI Hardware Design</strong>: Work on FPGA-based AI accelerators and neural network implementations
        - <strong>Embedded Systems</strong>: Develop IoT applications and embedded software/hardware co-design solutions
        - <strong>Edge AIoT</strong>: Build intelligent edge computing systems combining AI and Internet of Things
        - <strong>Generative AI</strong>: Explore large language models, diffusion models, and generative AI applications
        - <strong>Cybersecurity</strong>: Master security principles, threat analysis, and secure system design
        
        Students work on real research projects alongside our team, contributing to publications and gaining invaluable experience in academic research.
        </div>

        ### About the Gifted Education Fund
        
        The GEF encourages post-secondary institutions, non-government organisations (NGOs), professional bodies, and technology enterprises to provide diversified off-school advanced learning programmes. Programme providers receive funding support to deliver high-quality, challenging learning experiences for gifted students.
        
        ### CityUHK CALAS as Programme Provider
        
        City University of Hong Kong's Architecture Laboratory for Arithmetic and Security (CALAS) has been an approved programme provider since 2020. Our latest programme for the 2025/26 school year (Programme No. <strong>2025-06</strong>) continues our commitment to providing gifted students with advanced exposure to university-level research and hands-on experience in computer engineering.
