Dr. Lucas's paper on a high-performance CKKS key switching accelerator has been accepted by IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computing.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

Congratulations to **Dr. Lucas** on the acceptance of his paper, **"HENM: A High-Performance CKKS Key Switching Accelerator via Near-Memory Computing"**, by **IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computing (TETC)**.

The paper was accepted after a major revision, marking an important milestone in a rigorous review process. This achievement reflects Dr. Lucas's sustained effort and dedication throughout a long research journey.

The work advances high-performance acceleration for CKKS key switching through near-memory computing, contributing to efficient hardware support for privacy-preserving computation and homomorphic encryption.

Congratulations again to Dr. Lucas on this well-deserved achievement.
