We're delighted to congratulate Mehdi, Sanka, and Irfan on being granted a USA patent for their innovative work in medical diagnosis technology!
<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">
The patent, "Novel hardware design for neuropathy and myopathy diagnosis from EMG signal" (PWG/PA/1032/6/2021), represents a significant achievement in the field of biomedical engineering and hardware design. This innovative research addresses critical challenges in diagnosing neurological and muscular disorders through advanced EMG signal processing and custom hardware implementation.

Since the initial filing in 2021, this project has been supported by Prof. Hung, whose guidance has been instrumental throughout the patent application and review process. The successful grant of this USA patent reflects the team's dedication, technical excellence, and perseverance through the rigorous patent examination process.

This achievement serves as an important reminder that patent applications require significant time for processing and iteration. We encourage all researchers to plan ahead and remain patient throughout the intellectual property protection journey. The successful outcome demonstrates that thorough preparation and persistence in patent applications can lead to valuable recognition and protection of innovative research.

Congratulations to Mehdi, Sanka, and Irfan on this well-deserved achievement, and special thanks to Prof. Hung for his continued support!
