We are pleased to share that Leo has received CityUHK research activity funding for his Cornell exchange visit.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

Leo HUANG has received CityUHK research activity funding of HK$45,000 in support of his upcoming exchange visit to Cornell University. This funding is a strong recognition of the value of his exchange plan and will help support his academic and research activities during his time abroad.

Leo's Cornell exchange is an exciting opportunity to broaden his international research experience, deepen academic collaboration, and engage with leading research activities in his field. We are pleased to see this important milestone receive concrete support and encouragement.

This achievement also serves as a timely reminder to all CALAS members to plan ahead and take action when pursuing international exchange and research opportunities.

Congratulations again to Leo, and we wish him a highly rewarding and successful exchange visit at Cornell University.
