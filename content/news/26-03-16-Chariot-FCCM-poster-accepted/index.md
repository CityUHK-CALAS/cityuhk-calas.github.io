Please join us in congratulating Jerry, Leo, and Henry on their poster paper acceptance at IEEE FCCM 2026.

<!--more-->

Their paper, **"Chariot: Compiler-Aware Heterogeneous Graph Representation Learning for Automated HLS Optimization,"** has been accepted as a poster paper at **IEEE FCCM 2026**.

FCCM is one of the leading conferences in FPGA and custom computing research, and this acceptance highlights the team's strong work at the intersection of compiler techniques, graph representation learning, and automated HLS optimization.

Congratulations again to **Jerry**, **Leo**, and **Henry** on this excellent achievement. Keep going!
