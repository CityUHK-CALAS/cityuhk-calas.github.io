We are proud to announce that Prof. Ray will serve as Program Co-Chair at HEART 2026 in Germany!

<!--more-->
![](image1.png)
<div style="text-align: justify">

Congratulations to Prof. Ray on being appointed as Program Co-Chair for the International Symposium on Highly Efficient Accelerators and Reconfigurable Technologies (HEART 2026)! This prestigious symposium will be held at Heidelberg University, Germany, from June 17-19, 2026.

HEART is a premier forum for presenting and discussing cutting-edge research on computing systems utilizing acceleration technology. The symposium focuses on achieving high efficiency through accelerators, tailored hardware architectures, and innovative computing methods across a wide spectrum of systemsâ€”from high-performance computing and data centers to mobile and IoT applications.

All CALAS members are encouraged to prepare and submit papers to this exciting venue. Accepted papers will be published in the ACM International Conference Proceedings Series (ICPS) and will appear in the ACM Digital Library with open access.

**Important Dates:**
- Submissions due: March 13, 2026 (AoE)
- Notification: April 17, 2026 (AoE)
- Conference Dates: June 17-19, 2026

For more information and submission details, please visit the [HEART 2026 website](https://heart2026.github.io/). You can also check the [committee list](https://heart2026.github.io/Committees.html) to see potential future examiners in the field.

Let's get ready and submit our papers!
