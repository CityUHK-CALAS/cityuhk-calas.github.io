We are delighted to share that Jimmy JING has achieved a new full paper acceptance at IEEE SMC 2026.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

Please join us in congratulating Jimmy JING on the acceptance of his full paper, **"Efficient Progressive Pre-training of Language Models by Aligning Model Growth with Dataset Complexity,"** at the **2026 IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics (SMC)**.

IEEE SMC is a flagship international conference in the field, and the 2026 edition will be held on **October 4-7, 2026** in **Bellevue, WA, USA**. This acceptance is a meaningful recognition of Jimmy's research effort and continued progress.

Jimmy also shared his appreciation to Prof. Ray and all CALAS members for their support and encouragement. He especially thanked Ashraf and Meng for their help on the paper, and noted that the team is extending this work toward a journal article.

We are proud to see Jimmy continue building strong momentum in his PhD journey. Congratulations again to Jimmy on this excellent achievement.

Conference website: [IEEE SMC 2026](https://www.ieeesmc2026.org)
