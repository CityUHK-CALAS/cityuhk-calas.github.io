Claire's paper on operator-blind secret mediation for AI agents has been accepted by Mathematics.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

Congratulations to **Claire JIN** on the acceptance of her paper, **"Operator-Blind Secret Mediation for AI Agents: A Formal Model and FHE Construction for Credential Derivation on Untrusted Infrastructure"**, by **Mathematics**.

This work develops a formal model and fully homomorphic encryption (FHE)-based construction for credential derivation in untrusted infrastructure, addressing an important security and privacy challenge for AI agents.

The acceptance reflects Claire's sustained research effort and the valuable support from **Prof. Ray**, **Prof. Patrick**, **Prof. Winston**, **Prof. Donald**, and CALAS members throughout the research journey.

Congratulations again to Claire on this fruitful achievement.
