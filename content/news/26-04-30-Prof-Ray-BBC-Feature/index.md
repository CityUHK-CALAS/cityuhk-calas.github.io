We are pleased to share that Prof. Ray has been featured by BBC Chinese in a report examining how artificial intelligence is transforming education and how students, parents, and educators are adapting to this rapidly changing landscape.

In the article, Prof. Ray discusses how educational reform should not simply chase fast technological changes, but instead focus on a more fundamental shift from a teaching-centered approach to a learning-centered one. He also highlights the importance of smaller-group interaction, deeper engagement among students, and the thoughtful integration of AI as a collaborative partner in the classroom.

His remarks offer a clear and forward-looking perspective on how education can evolve in the AI era while keeping student learning at the center.

The BBC Chinese report is available here: [AI衝擊教育：中國家長怕孩子成了「科舉被廢後的秀才」](https://www.bbc.com/zhongwen/articles/clypne023mgo/trad).
