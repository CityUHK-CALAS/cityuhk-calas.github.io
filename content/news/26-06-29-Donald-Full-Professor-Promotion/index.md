Prof. Donald Chen has been promoted to Full Professor at BNU-HKBU United International College.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

CALAS warmly congratulates **Prof. Donald Chen** on his promotion to **Full Professor** at **BNU-HKBU United International College (UIC)**.

This wonderful news marks an important milestone in Prof. Donald's academic career and reflects his sustained contributions to research, teaching, and scholarly leadership. As a CALAS PhD graduate, his achievement is also a proud moment for the wider CALAS community.

With Prof. David and Prof. Donald now serving as full professors, CALAS is delighted to see its PhD graduates continuing to make meaningful impact as prominent faculty members and academic leaders.

Congratulations to Prof. Donald on this well-deserved promotion.
