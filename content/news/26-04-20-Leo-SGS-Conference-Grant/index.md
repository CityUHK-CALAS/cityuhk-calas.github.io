We are pleased to share that Leo has received the CityUHK SGS 2026 Conference Grant of $10,000 for attending FCCM in Atlanta, USA.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

We are pleased to announce that Leo has received the CityUHK SGS 2026 Conference Grant, valued at $10,000, in support of his attendance at the FCCM conference in Atlanta, USA, from May 13 to May 16.

This grant recognizes Leo's research progress and the value of participating in a leading international conference. Attending FCCM will provide an excellent opportunity to engage with the research community, exchange ideas with experts in the field, and further strengthen CALAS's international visibility.

We extend our sincere congratulations to Leo on this well-deserved achievement and wish him a fruitful and rewarding conference experience in Atlanta.
