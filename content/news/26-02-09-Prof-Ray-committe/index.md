Congratulations to Prof. Ray on being appointed to the CODES 2026 Program Committee!
<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

We are pleased to announce that Prof. Ray has been invited to serve on the program committee for the 2026 IEEE/ACM International Conference on Codesign of Embedded Systems (CODES 2026). This prestigious conference is a premier venue for presenting cutting-edge research in hardware-software co-design and embedded systems.

Being selected for the program committee is a significant recognition of Prof. Ray's expertise and contributions to the field of embedded systems research. As a committee member, he will play a vital role in shaping the technical program by reviewing submissions and helping to identify impactful research that advances the state of the art.

Congratulations once again to Prof. Ray on this well-deserved appointment! This achievement reflects the continued excellence and growing influence of CALAS in the international research community.
</div>
