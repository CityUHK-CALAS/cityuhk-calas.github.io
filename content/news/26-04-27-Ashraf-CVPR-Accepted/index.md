We are pleased to announce that Dr. Ashraf's paper, "Online Interpretable Matrix Decomposition for Large-Scale Streaming Data," has been accepted by CVPR.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

We are pleased to announce that Dr. Ashraf's paper, **"Online Interpretable Matrix Decomposition for Large-Scale Streaming Data,"** has been accepted by CVPR, one of the leading international conferences in artificial intelligence and computer vision.

This acceptance is a significant academic achievement and a strong recognition of the quality, originality, and relevance of his research. Having work accepted by a top-tier venue such as CVPR reflects both the technical depth of the contribution and its potential impact on the broader AI research community.

The paper addresses an important problem in large-scale streaming data analysis, with a focus on online and interpretable matrix decomposition. The topic is timely and meaningful, especially as the demand for scalable, transparent, and efficient machine learning methods continues to grow across data-intensive applications.

We extend our sincere congratulations to Dr. Ashraf on this excellent result. This accomplishment highlights his continued dedication to high-quality research, and we look forward to seeing the further impact of this work.
