Prof. Mehdi and Team CALAS have published a collaborative systematic review paper in SN Computer Science.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

Congratulations to **Prof. Mehdi Hasan Chowdhury** and Team CALAS on the publication of their collaborative review article, **"Electrooculogram Based Point of Care Systems: A Systematic Review"**, in **SN Computer Science**.

Published by Springer Nature on **29 June 2026**, the article reviews recent progress in electrooculogram (EOG)-based point-of-care systems, with attention to hardware implementations, signal processing methods, classification approaches, system performance, and future research directions.

This publication reflects a strong collaborative effort involving Prof. Mehdi, **Dr. Abdurrashid Ibrahim Sanka**, **Prof. Ray C. C. Cheung**, and the wider research team. It also highlights CALAS's continued contributions to point-of-care technologies, biosignal processing, and reconfigurable computing for healthcare applications.

Congratulations again to Prof. Mehdi and all co-authors on this achievement.

Source: [Springer Nature](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s42979-026-05203-1)
