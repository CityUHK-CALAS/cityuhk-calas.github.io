Please join us in congratulating Simon and Henry on their new full paper accepted at IEEE FCCM 2026, a Tier-1 FPGA conference with an acceptance rate of around 15%.

<!--more-->

Their paper, **"ViM-Q: Scalable Algorithm-Hardware Co-Design for Vision Mamba Model Inference on FPGA"**, has been accepted by **IEEE FCCM 2026**.

FCCM, the **34th IEEE International Symposium on Field-Programmable Custom Computing Machines**, is one of the premier venues in FPGA and custom computing research. Having a full paper accepted there is a strong recognition of the novelty and quality of the team's work.

This result highlights Simon and Henry's excellent effort in algorithm-hardware co-design for efficient Vision Mamba inference on FPGA. It is also a great demonstration of what can be achieved with a powerful FPGA board and solid end-to-end system design.

Congratulations again to **Simon** and **Henry** on this outstanding achievement.
