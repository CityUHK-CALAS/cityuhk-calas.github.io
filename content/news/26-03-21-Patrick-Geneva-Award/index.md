Congratulations to Prof. Patrick Hung for receiving a Silver Award at the International Exhibition of Inventions of Geneva for the project "High-Performance Artificial Intelligence Server Information System."

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

The International Exhibition of Inventions of Geneva is one of the world's most prestigious events dedicated to innovation, attracting inventors, researchers, and industry leaders from across the globe each year. Prof. Patrick's award-winning project addresses the growing demand for high-performance computing infrastructure tailored to artificial intelligence workloads, presenting a novel server information system architecture that delivers superior efficiency and scalability.

This Silver Award is a testament to Prof. Patrick's longstanding expertise in computer architecture and high-performance computing, and it further reinforces the impact of CALAS-affiliated research on the international stage. We extend our warmest congratulations to Prof. Patrick on this outstanding achievement.
