We are pleased to share that Jerry ZHANG and the team have achieved a new paper acceptance in IEEE Sensors Journal.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

We are delighted to announce that the paper "High-Fidelity Conditional Side-Channel Trace Synthesis using Diffusion Models" has been accepted by IEEE Sensors Journal.

Congratulations to Jerry ZHANG and Donald on this excellent achievement, and also to Winston and Cetin for their contributions as co-authors. This is a meaningful recognition of the team's continued efforts in side-channel security research and generative AI.

The work explores the use of diffusion models to synthesize high-fidelity conditional side-channel traces, advancing research at the intersection of hardware security, machine learning, and signal analysis. Its acceptance by IEEE Sensors Journal reflects both the technical quality and the growing relevance of this research direction.

As Prof. Ray highlighted, strong publications become important building blocks in one's academic journey. We are glad to see Jerry moving forward steadily and building a strong research profile through sustained effort and teamwork.

Congratulations again to Jerry and the whole team.

Source: [IEEE Sensors Journal official page](https://ieee-sensors.org/ieee-sensors-journal/).
