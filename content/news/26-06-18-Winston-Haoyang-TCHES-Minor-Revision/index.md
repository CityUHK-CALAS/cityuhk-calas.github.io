Prof. Winston and Haoyang have received a minor revision from TCHES for their paper on high-throughput GPU design for MAYO.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

We are pleased to announce that **Prof. Winston** and his student **Haoyang** have received a minor revision from Transactions on Cryptographic Hardware and Embedded Systems (TCHES) for their paper, **"High-Throughput GPU Design and Implementation of MAYO with Matrix Computation Reordering"**.

The work presents a high-throughput GPU design and implementation of MAYO, a post-quantum digital signature scheme, with matrix computation reordering to improve computational efficiency.

This progress reflects the team's continued contributions to efficient hardware and software implementation for post-quantum cryptography. Congratulations to Prof. Winston and Haoyang on this achievement.
