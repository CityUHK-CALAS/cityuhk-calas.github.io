CALAS has secured a new CityUHK Strategic Seed Fund for research on reconfigurable privacy-preserving AI computing.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

CALAS has recently secured a new CityUHK Strategic Seed Fund for the project **"Reconfigurable Privacy-Preserving AI Computing and Acceleration Platform for Secure Cross-Institutional Collaboration"**.

This project will support research on secure and efficient AI computing platforms, with a focus on privacy-preserving computation, reconfigurable acceleration, and trusted collaboration across institutions. The funding provides valuable support for advancing CALAS's ongoing work at the intersection of AI, hardware acceleration, and secure computing.

Congratulations to our postdoctoral team and PhD students for their contributions to this achievement. We look forward to the continued progress and impact of this research direction.
