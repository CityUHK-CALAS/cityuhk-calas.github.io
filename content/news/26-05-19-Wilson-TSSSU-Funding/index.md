<div style="text-align: justify">

An exciting new milestone has arrived for Wilson Wong. His venture has been awarded <strong>HK$1.4 million</strong> in new support through the <strong>TSSSU</strong>, marking an important step forward in its continued development.

Wilson has consistently demonstrated strong initiative in translating research and engineering ideas into practical innovation. This new funding is a meaningful recognition of both the venture's potential and the value of sustained effort in building technology with real-world impact.

As Wilson noted, this funding is also a collaboration with CALAS, and he is happy to bring new value to the group. It is encouraging to see this connection between research, innovation, and entrepreneurship continue to grow.

Congratulations again to Wilson on this encouraging achievement. We look forward to seeing the next stage of development for his venture and the broader impact it can create.
