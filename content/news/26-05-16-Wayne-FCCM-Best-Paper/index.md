We warmly congratulate Prof. Wayne Luk on receiving the Best Paper Award at IEEE FCCM 2026.

<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

We warmly congratulate our grand supervisor, Prof. Wayne Luk, on receiving the <strong>Best Paper Award</strong> at <strong>IEEE FCCM 2026</strong>, the 34th IEEE International Symposium on Field-Programmable Custom Computing Machines, held in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, from May 13 to 16, 2026.

The award was presented for the outstanding paper <strong>"HGQ-LUT: Fast LUT-Aware Training and Efficient Architectures for DNN Inference"</strong>, which was recognized for its excellent contribution to FPGA and custom computing research.

This achievement reflects the high impact of Prof. Wayne Luk's research leadership and the strength of the collaboration behind the work. It is always inspiring to see such distinguished recognition at one of the premier conferences in the FPGA community.

Congratulations again to Prof. Wayne Luk and the full author team on this well-deserved honor. We are proud to celebrate this exciting milestone and look forward to more outstanding achievements ahead.

</div>
