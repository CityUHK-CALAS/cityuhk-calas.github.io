Please also join us in congratulating Dr. Yile, Ryan, Dr. Sanka, Dr. Lucas, and Prof. Donald on their journal paper acceptance in Optics Letters.

<!--more-->

Their paper, **"NZ-OVA: Non-Zero-Padding Overlap-Add Method for Chromatic Dispersion Compensation,"** has been accepted for publication in **Optics Letters**.

This acceptance is a strong recognition of the team's contribution to optical communication research. The work on non-zero-padding overlap-add processing for chromatic dispersion compensation highlights a practical and technically meaningful advance in the field.
