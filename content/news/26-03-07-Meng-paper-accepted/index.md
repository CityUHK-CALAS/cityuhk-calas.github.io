Congrats to Meng, Ashraf, and Jimmy on their paper accepted in Neurocomputing (IF 6.5)!
<!--more-->

<div style="text-align: justify">

Congratulations to **Meng, Ashraf, and Jimmy** for their paper **"Memory-Efficient Neural Network Training via Gradient Compression through Continuous Basis Tracking"** being accepted in **Neurocomputing** (Impact Factor: 6.5)!

This research tackles the critical challenge of memory efficiency in neural network training by leveraging gradient compression through continuous basis tracking — an elegant approach that reduces memory footprint without sacrificing model accuracy. The journey was not short: the work began in February last year, was submitted in September, and went through one round of rigorous revision and resubmission before earning its well-deserved acceptance. Special thanks to **Prof. Ray** and **Prof. Yan** for their invaluable supervision and guidance throughout the process, and to Ashraf and Jimmy for their wonderful teamwork and contributions. Keep up the excellent work!
